1.  **Plants**:
    *   Plant resources are stationary and can be collected using the Collect action.
    *   Each plant restores 10 HP when consumed.

2.  **Prey Animals**:
    *   Prey animals are mobile resources that require hunting.
    *   Each prey animal has 4 HP and can fight back, dealing 1 damage to you if the hunt fails.
    *   Successful hunting yields meat, which is more nutritious than plants (20 HP per meat unit).
    *   Each 2 points of prey HP converts to 1 unit of meat in your inventory.
    *   Hunting is riskier but more rewarding than plant collection.
