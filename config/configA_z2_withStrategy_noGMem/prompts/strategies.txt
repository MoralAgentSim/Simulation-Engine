1. With more inventories, it’ll help not only you but also your family and others when in need. It may be wise to collect and hunt as much as possible.
2. Collecting plant doesn’t have risks, meanwhile plants takes many steps to respawn when it's depleted. It is a safe and scarse low hanging fruit.
3. Hunting together can reduce risk - since it’ll kill an animal in shorter rounds and will incur fewer chance of being fight back. 
4. The last one who strike the animal to death gets all the meat. So even if people collaborated on hunting, the resource distribution is not automatically based on the contribution. It is totally depend on the person who gets it - he might share or not share, fairly or unfairly. It is wise to pay attention to what kind of person he is, or take some actions toward him based on his distribution.
